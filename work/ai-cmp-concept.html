Case study

AI-first CMP concept

Exploratory prototype for AI-powered content planning and ideation — a discovery artefact to align Product Management on what “AI-native” could mean beyond incremental features.

Sitecore AI Discovery

1 · Business problem

From AI features to AI-shaped products

Incremental AI buttons inside legacy CMP workflows may not change how marketing teams plan content. Leadership needed a tangible vision for a net-new experience — planning, ideation, and orchestration with AI at the centre — to stress-test strategy before committing engineering orgs.

2 · Approach

Demo to facilitate decisions

I built a clickable concept to run structured discovery with PMs: what jobs would move here, what stays in authoring tools, what data the product must own.

AI-first CMP prototype (Figma) →

3 · Outcomes

Clarity before build

The deliverable was alignment — a shared reference for scope, differentiation, and sequencing. Not every concept ships; the business value is avoiding expensive wrong bets.
