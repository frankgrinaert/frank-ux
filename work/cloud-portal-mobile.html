Case study

Cloud Portal — mobile optimisation

Made the portal usable on small screens by fixing components in the design system — not redrawing every screen one by one.

Sitecore Responsive design Design specs

1 · Business problem

Mobile usage was real; the UI was not

Enterprise buyers still open portals on phones — for alerts, quick checks, approvals. A desktop-only Cloud Portal undermined the “modern platform” promise and created support noise when layouts broke on narrow viewports.

Screen-by-screen redesign would never keep pace with a multi-product suite. The business needed leverage: fix once in the system, improve many surfaces.

2 · Approach

Atomic responsiveness in Blok

Instead of bespoke page mocks, I specified responsive behaviour per component — homepage layout, tile grids, banners — so engineering could roll fixes across Blok consumers.

This multiplied impact: one spec pattern repaired dozens of screens in multiple products.
